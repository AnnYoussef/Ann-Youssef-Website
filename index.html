Ann is an accomplished and dedicated optometrist with a passion for improving the vision and overall eye health of her patients. With a strong educational background and years of experience in the field, Ann has become a trusted professional in the optometry community. Her journey is marked by numerous career highlights and a commitment to providing exceptional eye care to her patients.

Ann earned her Doctor of Optometry (OD) degree from the renowned University of California, Riverside (UCR), where she was not only a diligent student but also an active participant in various eye research projects. Her educational background at UCR laid a solid foundation for her career in optometry, instilling in her a deep appreciation for the importance of eye health.

After completing her degree, Ann embarked on her professional journey as an optometrist. She started her career in a private practice, where she honed her clinical skills and developed a reputation for her compassionate and patient-centered approach to eye care. Over the years, she has made it her mission to stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in optometry to provide the best possible care to her patients. One of Ann's career highlights was her role in a community outreach program in which she organized free eye screenings and eyeglasses distribution for underserved populations. Her dedication to making a positive impact on her community through vision care earned her recognition and respect from her peers and patients alike
